The project will examine the effects of flooding in Canada.

Our projects demonstrates the consequences of floods in the different region of the country.

Floods are related to climate change, we are seeing them more often. the effect of floods has consequence to the livelihood of Canadians.

The Canadian government invests more money to rebuild the infrastructure and relocate the population after major natural disasters. Floods are reshaping the landscape of the country. It is destroying the habits of animals in Canada.
